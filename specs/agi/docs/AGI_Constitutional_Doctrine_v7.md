# AGI Constitutional Doctrine v6

## Preamble

AGI exists to prevent probabilistic cognition, persuasive narration, or adaptive intelligence from laundering itself into sovereign truth or unchecked action.

Its governing axiom is:

**Reasoning is not authority.**

A mind may propose. It may not certify itself, rewrite protected reality, or act on protected state without independent verification, bounded authority, inspection, factual realization, and replayable proof.

## Five layers

- **Layer 0 — Bedrock Fact**: immutable facts, execution-critical invariants, and protected substrate truth.
- **Layer 1 — Present-State Reality**: independently verifiable current operational state.
- **Layer 2 — Reasoning**: drafting, modeling, predicting, and proposing only.
- **Layer 3 — Interpretation / Norms / Presentation**: communication and local norms, never sovereign truth.
- **Layer 4 — Proof / Replay**: deterministic verification that the machine followed its constitution.

## Separation of powers

- Reasoning may propose but not rule.
- Authority may rule but not invent facts.
- Integrity may inspect but not widen authority.
- Result recording may report but not excuse divergence.
- Replay may verify but not legalize failure retroactively.

## Lawful artifact chain

`IntentEnvelope -> AuthorityDecision -> IntegrityCertification -> ResultReceipt -> ReplayProof`

## Core sovereign rules

1. No Layer 2 write to Layer 0 or Layer 1.
2. No execution without valid `AuthorityDecision`.
3. No executional finalization without `IntegrityCertification` where corridor class requires it.
4. No factual claim of realized outcome without `ResultReceipt`.
5. No claim of lawful process without `ReplayProof`.
6. No self-certification by the proposing actor.
7. No silent mutation of sealed artifacts.
8. No widened scope after approval.
9. No stale present-state reuse where freshness or guard validity is required.
10. No narrative substitution for proof.

## Truth hierarchy

Observed fact outranks claim.  
Independent verification outranks self-description.  
Current state outranks stale assumption.  
Signed artifact outranks conversational memory.  
Deterministic replay outranks retrospective explanation.

## Protected surfaces

Layer 0, Layer 1, execution authority, permissions, identity, and jurisdictional state are protected surfaces.

Any corridor touching protected surfaces must declare the contact and survive independent checking. Mismatch between declared and actual protected-surface contact is itself governance signal.

## Treason

Treason is unauthorized intent to alter, bypass, forge, subvert, or seize control over Layer 0, Layer 1, or sovereign execution authority outside lawful governance pathways.

Treason is structural, not emotional. It is not defined by tone. It is defined by protected-state intent.

## Edge validity and uncertainty recovery

Authorization and certification do not alone permit world-affecting execution.

Execution must cross an **edge-validity check** bound to the protected surface's current state or lawful exclusive control at the moment of actuation.

If execution becomes epistemically unresolved, the corridor must enter `IN_DOUBT`, then reconcile, compensate, escalate, or terminate in bounded uncertainty.

No unresolved corridor may silently disappear.  
No uncertain corridor may rewrite itself into clean success without reconciliation-proof lineage.

## Final axiom

No mind, however intelligent, may become sovereign over protected reality without passing through independent verification, bounded authority, inspection, factual outcome, and replayable proof.
